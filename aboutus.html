With languages, you are at home anywhere

L’Avenir means the future & is thus named keeping in mind that language learning is the first step towards a golden future in a globalized economy. Our logo represents the globe inspiring us to be the pioneers in teaching all major languages spoken worldwide.

Vision & Mission


Vision:

▶ To be a world leader in providing solutions to any language related requirements
▶ To develop global competence skills and international mindedness
▶ To unify & empower people with linguistic, intercultural competence & international mindedness in a globalized world.

Mission:

▶ Provide a stimulating & interactive learning environment
▶ Contribute towards shaping culturally sensitized global citizens
▶ Transform communities by connecting people through language
▶ Create significant opportunities for personal & professional growth
