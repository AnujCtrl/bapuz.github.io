L'Avenir is helping people experience the sheer pleasure of learning.

L'Avenir is a language learning school that is the opposite of textbook learning: it’s enjoyable and effective. Founded in 2010, L'Avenir has quickly grown and now more than 50 million people in 189 countries learn languages with us to better themselves, connect with others and with the world around them.
